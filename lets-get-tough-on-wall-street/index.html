Let's Get Tough on Wall Street

Maureen Dowd says it nicely in her editorial in the New York Times. If you didn’t read the post already, you should. America needs to get upset, take action, and save the proletariat—not the fat cat. If the big banks fail, the free market will provide and there’s no shortage of smart people looking to make banking salaries. These guys don’t know anything special, well not in terms of stock trends at least—I doubt you know about million dollar bonuses, private jets, and $100+ bottles of wine :)
